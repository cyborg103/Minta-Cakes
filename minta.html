OUR STORY
We began our wonderful journey in Wollongong, Australia, from a small and cozy family bakery in the early 1970's. From generation to generation, our family, the Minta, has preserved a secret recipe for our savoury cakes. We offer two types of cakes:

♥  Season&Time  ♥
With four Seasons - Winter, Fall, Summer, and Spring;
and two Times - Day and Night
♥  Crystal Sea  ♥
A traditional specialty

Each keeps inside a whole dreamy season, and time, and tears, and sea, and love... Each has its own story, its own life.

OUR COURSES
———✤———

Our Cake Making Courses is the perfect place for any cake-making lovers!

Your instructor will show you the right way to bake and decorate cakes, step by step, so that you experience the excitement of successfully making your own cakes. You will understand how to bake a great cake, see how to make and color icing and learn the best way to ice the cake. You will also practice from the fundamentals to the advanced of decorating, enabling you to approach each technique the right way for great results.

The courses are divided into three levels -
Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced.

We open a new class for each level every three months.
